Welcome

My name is Dr. Miguel A. Moreno, I'm and Atomic and Space Physicist and a tenured Professor of Physics and Astronomy. In this program, I review the different methods and programs for the search for life in the Universe, from microorganisms to intelligent technological communicative civilizations.

Overview

Search in our solar system by sending robots to Mars, Europa, Titan, and Enceladus, comets and asteroids in search for water and microorganisms. A Theory of Life on Mars and other places in our solar system.
